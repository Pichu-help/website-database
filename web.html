Basic Introduction

For me, I'm very passionate about journalling .
I think of it as an act of self care. If you do not know what that is then here is the basic definition on journalling:

As you can see, journalling is a practice of writing down your own thoughts and feelings to understand yourself clearly. Especially if you are struggling with mental health, keeping a journal might be a good idea. Even so, it does not need to only contain your worries or dark secrets, you can also write down goals that you may want to achieve.

This website will provide you tips and advice on how to begin journalling and some goals to set.
